Our Story

Our love story began in back in 2011 while working at one of the most romantic places in all of Dubuque, IA…Texas Roadhouse! After a couple flirtatious exchanges, a few line dances and thousands of buns with cinnamon butter later, Daniel finally worked up the courage to ask Kassy on a date and the rest is history.

7 years worth of memories later, we’ve travelled the country, purchased our dream home and introduced a furry pup to our family. In December of 2017, Daniel finally popped the question on the Mississippi Riverwalk in front of the Stone Cliff Winery, and Kassy said yes! We can’t wait for this exciting new chapter to begin and we are so excited to celebrate with you on April 13th!
